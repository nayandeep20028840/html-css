About Us

SciFurniture are pioneers in the field of manufacturing of Plywood and Veneer products. We provide quality furniture at affordable prices. SciFurniture outlets are based in New York, San Francisco, and Salt Lake City.

Mission

The organization is dedicated to continuous research and development of new designs to create products for your comfort. It is a one-stop shop for all furniture related needs of homes.
