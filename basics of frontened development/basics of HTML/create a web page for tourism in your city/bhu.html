BHU Campus

Banaras Hindu University (BHU), formerly Central Hindu College, is a collegiate, central, research university located in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, India. It was established in 1916 jointly by Pandit Madan Mohan Malaviya,[6] the Maharaja of Darbhanga Rameshwar Singh,[6] Maharaja of Banaras Prabhu Narayan Singh,[6] Sunder Lal[6] and British Theosophist and Home Rule League founder Annie Besant.[7] With over 30,000 students residing on campus, it is one of the largest residential university in Asia.[8]The university is one of the ten public universities declared as an Institute of Eminence.
